Professional Overview

Mohammed Mostofa Kamal is the esteemed founder and Head of Chamber at Kamal & Associates, bringing over 25 years of distinguished legal experience to the firm. As a Senior Advocate of the Supreme Court of Bangladesh, he has established himself as a leading figure in criminal defense and corporate law.

His extensive career includes representing high-profile clients in complex criminal cases, corporate litigation, and constitutional matters. Advocate Kamal's strategic approach, combined with his deep understanding of Bangladesh's legal system, has resulted in numerous successful outcomes for his clients.
