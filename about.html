Our Story

A Legacy of Legal Excellence

Founded in 1985 by Senior Advocate Kamal Hossain, Kamal & Associates has grown from a small practice into one of Bangladesh's most respected full-service law firms. Our journey has been marked by unwavering commitment to justice, integrity, and client success.

Over four decades, we have built a reputation for excellence in legal representation, serving individuals, corporations, and institutions across all major practice areas. Our team of 50+ experienced attorneys brings diverse expertise and a shared dedication to achieving the best outcomes for our clients.
